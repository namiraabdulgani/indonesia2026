Indonesia's largest Hindu temple complex, three towering spires of Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva. Cooler and quieter in the afternoon. Outer grounds are flat and stroller-friendly. Hire a guide — the Ramayana mythology is spectacular.

★ Hue Imperial Citadel
Imperial City · Nguyen Dynasty Palace Complex
The heart of ancient Hue — walled imperial compound with Thai Hoa Palace, gates, pavilions, and courtyards. UNESCO World Heritage Site. A combo ticket covers this and the royal tombs. Morning is best — cooler and less crowded.

★ Khai Dinh Tomb
Khai Dinh Royal Tomb
The most dramatic of the Nguyen royal tombs — built with reinforced concrete and decorated entirely with intricate porcelain and glass mosaic. A unique East-West fusion unlike any other site in Vietnam. 127 steps up the hillside — worth every one.
📍 Chau Chu Mountain · ~10km from city center 🕐 7:00 am – 5:00 pm 🎟 150,000 VND adult (or use combo ticket) 💵 Cash only
