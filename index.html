★ Borobudur Sunset Experience
Register at Manohara Gate 7 · Sunset on the Temple
Exclusive late-entry access to Borobudur at golden hour — the temple glows as the sun sets over the Kedu Valley and Mount Merapi. Guided experience on the upper stupas. Limited to 100 people per day. Sura is free. Kai at child rate (Indonesian). Book well in advance via ticket.borobudurpark.com.

Day 03
Culture, Shopping & Prambanan
Wed · Sep 23
Day 3 Journey · Keraton → Wijilan → Malioboro → Prambanan → Abhayagiri
09:30
★ Cultural Show
Keraton Yogyakarta · Wayang Golek
Wednesday show is Wayang Golek (Menak Puppet) at 9:30am — arrive by 9:00. Shaded pavilions are great for Tim and Susan. Sura in carrier. Museum galleries after. Allow 1.5 hrs for the group.
📍 Keraton Yogyakarta 🎭 Wayang Golek · Wed 9:30am IDR 25K adult · IDR 20K child (Kai) 🔍 Verify: kratonjogja.id
10:45
Shopping
Museum Sonobudoyo · Wayang & Art
Short walk from Keraton on the same square. Finest wayang kulit puppets, masks, and batik in the city — watch artisans making puppets from buffalo hide. Best place in Jogja for meaningful art to bring home.
📍 Museum Sonobudoyo 🎭 Authentic wayang kulit shop 👁 Watch artisans at work
12:00
Lunch
Jalan Wijilan · Gudeg Bu Rini or Bu Lies
2-minute walk from Keraton. Bu Rini and Bu Lies are praised for cleanliness and sit-down setting — safe for bule stomachs (T.K., Tim, Susan). Classic gudeg: jackfruit stew, rice, egg, krecek. Mild and sweet enough for Kai.
📍 Jalan Wijilan, near Keraton ✅ Bule-belly safe · clean kitchen 🍚 Gudeg — Jogja's signature dish
13:00
Coffee Break
Kopi Pakpos Nol Km
Right at Zero Kilometer Point — vintage Dutch-colonial café, great coffee. Perfect rest while Sura feeds and Kai resets before the shopping stretch.
📍 Zero Kilometer, Yogyakarta
13:30
Shopping
Hamzah Batik · Beringharjo · Tim in van
Tim rests in the AC van while Namira, T.K., Susan, and Kai shop. Hamzah Batik is air-conditioned with fixed prices — great for stress-free batik shopping. Beringharjo upper floors for the best tulis batik at negotiable prices.
📍 Hamzah Batik, Malioboro 📍 Beringharjo Market 🛍 Hamzah: AC · fixed price 💵 Cash only in market
14:30
Optional
Kota Gede · Silver District
If energy allows — Jl. Kemasan is lined with silver workshops. Tom's Silver and HS Silver have fixed prices and purity certificates. Watch artisans hand-beat silver into wayang figurines and jewellery. Skip if the group is tired — Prambanan is the priority.
📍 Kota Gede Silver District ⏱ 45 min max 💍 Tom's Silver · HS Silver
15:15
★ Prambanan Temple
Prambanan · All Attend · Afternoon Visit
All six attend — Tim included. Indonesia's largest Hindu temple complex, three towering spires of Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva. Cooler and quieter in the afternoon. Buggy service for Tim and Susan. Outer grounds flat and stroller-friendly for Sura. Hire a guide — the Ramayana mythology is spectacular for Kai too.
📍 Prambanan Temple Complex IDR 400K: T.K., Tim, Susan · IDR 225K: Kai · Sura free 🚌 Buggy service for Tim & Susan 🧳 Stroller-friendly outer grounds
17:30
★ Sunset Dinner
Abhayagiri · Sumberwatu Heritage Resort
5-minute drive uphill from Prambanan. Panoramic views of the temple spires and Mount Merapi at golden hour — a perfect family moment. Indonesian and international menu. Reserve a family table with a view in advance.
📍 Abhayagiri, Sumberwatu 🌅 Sunset over Prambanan + Merapi 📞 Reserve: ask for view + family table 🕐 Open until 21:00
